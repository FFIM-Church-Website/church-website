Our Founders

The Fruitful Family International Ministry was born when the lord spoke to Presiding Bishop and Rev. Mrs. Agyei Frimpong to raise for him a generation of Believers who prize God's word above all things. The Lord reitirated to them the problem of the Church: Ignorance, as stated in HOSEA 4:6. The Lord said to them, "I made life to be enjoyed, not to be endured and yet many of my children are only struggling through life." The Presiding Bishop and his Wife discovered from the Scriptures that from the beginning God blessed man to be fruitful and productive in life (Genesis 1:28). Jesus, our Lord and Saviour, also came that we might have and enjoy life (John 10:10). However it takes Revelation knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ for any man to have and enjoy this fruitful life. Presiding Bishop and Mama understood that every man needs to kmow why Jesus came, what His suffering, death and resurrection have accomplished for all mankind. This, then became passion, to bring the knowledge of the benefits of Christ's vicarious sacrifice to all. This is the reason for establishing the Fruitful Family International Ministry.

Fruitful Family International Ministry is one Church in many locations

Fruitful Family International Ministry is an end time ministry born of the Holy Spirit to prepare a triumphant Church for the coming of the lord Jesus Christ. By a triumphant church, we mean a church that is awakened unto the reality of her identity in Christ. By the death and resurrection of Jesus Christ, our Lord, God has brought us into union with himself. We have heirs of God and joint-heirs with Christ. We are no longer sinners who are under curses or the devil, but have become the bona-fide sons of God. We were called to live victorious and triumphant lives. We were called for the glorified life. A life fruitfullness and prductivity. However, nobody can live above the level of his knowledge; every man's life is a reflection of what he knows. This is Why it is so imperative on us that we bring the knowledge of Christ's Salvation and its benefits to all, especially those in the church. God said in the book of HOSEA 4:6, that "his people are destroyed for the lack of knowledge." God was talking about Revelation Knowledge, not just Sense Knowledge.The Fruitful Family International Ministry therefore seeks to enrich all with the glorious Gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ. When you join this church you will learn of Jesus, why he came and what His vicarious sacrifice has accomplished for us. We also seek to help every Christian to identify their purpose in Christ and to live as true disciples of Christ. Loved one, May the Spirit of God Grant you insight into the plans and purposes of God in your life. We pray that you will find time to fellowship with us soon. REMAIN BLESSED!!!.
