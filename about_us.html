About Us

OUR HISTORY

The Fruitful Family International Ministry was born when the lord spoke to Presiding Bishop and Rev. Mrs. Agyei Frimpong to raise for a generation of Believers who prize God's word above all things. The Lord reitirated to them the problem of the Church: Ignorance, as stated in HOSEA 4:6. The Lord said to them, "I made life to be enjoyed, not to be endured and yet many of my children are only struggling through life." The Presiding Bishop and his Wife discovered from the Scriptures that from the beginning God blessed man to be fruitful and productive in life (Genesis 1:28). Jesus, our Lord and Saviour, also came that we might have and enjoy life (John 10:10). However it takes Revelation knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ for any man to have and enjoy this fruitful life. Presiding Bishop and Mama understood that every man needs to kmow why Jesus came, what His suffering, death and resurrection have accomplished for all mankind. This, then became passion, to bring the knowledge of the benefits of Christ's vicarious sacrifice to all. This is the reason for establishing the Fruitful Family International Ministry.The Church started in the living room of the Presiding Bishops's residence on the 18th of April 2014, with 12 members comprising of the Presiding Bishop's family and 7 neighbours. It was Easter Friday.The Fruitful life to manifest almost immediately. The Easter Sunday Service recorded 21 people in attendace. By the close of first month the Church had grown to over members. Week after week, the Church grew, and by the 10th week, there were about 100 people in attendance. A launching ceremony was then planned, to coincide with the moving of the Church to its current location. Canopies were rented and a grand launching ceremony was held was held which recorded over 400 people in attendance. The week after the launching, Church recorded 140 members. After three weeks of meeting under the canopies, a shed was erected. It is this shed which has been develped into our current auditorium. Currently the church has about 500 adult membership with over 250 children.

OUR VISION

Building a triumphant Church of enlightened believers who live Fruitful and Productive lives, displaying the glory of God everywhere through understanding of their identity in Christ.

OUR MISSION

Bringing appropriate and relevent knowledge of the benefits of Christ's Vicarious sacrifice to all, for a Fruitful and Productive life.
